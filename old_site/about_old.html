Me during my bachelor thesis discussion.
(Padua, october 2017)

I was born in Palmanova (Italy) in 1995.

I got a bachelor's and a master's degrees in Materials Science from Padua University in 2017 and in 2019, respectively.

From october 2019 to december 2022 I have been enrolled in the SIMN Doctoral Program under the supervision of Prof. Alberta Ferrarini and I am about to defend my PhD thesis titled Curvature, Flexibility and Orientational Fluctuations: how molecular features translate into elastic properties and mesoscale structure in Nematics.
